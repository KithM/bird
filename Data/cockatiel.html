The Cockatiel (Nymphicus hollandicus) is a beloved pet bird native to Australia, known for its distinctive crest and bright cheek patches. Typically living between 15-20 years with proper care, their diet mainly consists of seeds, but they also enjoy fresh fruits and vegetables. As sociable and trainable birds, Cockatiels thrive in environments that offer interaction, toys, and positive reinforcement. It's crucial to provide them with clean habitats, fresh water, and spacious cages for optimal well-being.
