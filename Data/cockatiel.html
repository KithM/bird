Cockatiel (Nymphicus hollandicus)

General Information

The Cockatiel is a popular pet bird native to Australia. They are known for their distinctive crests and bright cheek patches.

Lifespan

With proper care, a Cockatiel can live between 15-20 years, sometimes even longer.

Diet

Cockatiels are primarily seed-eaters, but they also benefit from a variety of fresh fruits and vegetables. Pelleted diets can also be a nutritious choice.

Care Tips

  • Provide a spacious cage for them to move around.
  • Offer toys and perches to keep them entertained.
  • Maintain a clean environment; change the cage lining regularly.
  • Provide fresh water daily.

Behavior and Training

Cockatiels are social birds and can be trained to perform tricks, mimic sounds, and even whistle tunes. Regular interaction and positive reinforcement are key to a happy and well-behaved Cockatiel.
